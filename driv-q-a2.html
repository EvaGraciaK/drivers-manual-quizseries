Cheating and Bribery

1. An open book within the applicant`s vision field, looking around, or checking a device during the exam

2. Failing the exam and being forbidden from taking the test again for 30 days

3. A fine and at least 7 days in jail; yes, both must be applied in every such situation

4. The State Secretary`s office will deny the applicant a driver`s license/permit

5. An employee of the State Secretary`s office, a commercial driver school owner, or anyone valid to instruct drivers or administer part of a driver`s license exam

Vision Screening

1. Get a vision screening, or submit a Vision Specialist Report

2. It must be completed by an eye doctor or physician, and it must be dated within 6 months of a facility visit to get your driver's license

3. At any local Driver Services facility, or at ilsos.gov

4. Minimum visual acuity of 20/40, and at least 140° peripheral vision, both requirements with or without corrective lenses

5. Ability to see to the side

6. The sharpness and clarity of one`s eyesight

7. They`ll be required to wear glasses or contact lenses while driving

8. Meet special requirements and do additional vision testing

Written Test

1. Every 8 years

2. Identify traffic signs, identify signals and pavement markings, and answer multi-choice and true or false questions about rules

3. By shape, color and symbol

4. Traffic laws, safety rules, crash prevention, and vehicle equipment

Driving Exam

1. 79 years old

2. Enrollment in driver`s school for adults

3.
  • It must be properly licensed and have the required equipment
  • be in the weight class of the license applications
  • be properly insured (you'll need to give proof of insurance)
  • show valid Illinois front and back license plates and registration stickers
  • and be driven to a facility by a driver with a valid driver`s license/permit.

4. Only the examiner

5. Bring someone to watch over and care for them

6. Commit a traffic violation or do something dangerous

7. Checking vehicle controls, backing the vehicle 50 ft, do a turnabout using an alley on the left side of the street, park uphill on the side of a road, start uphill from a parked position, park downhill on the side of the road, start downhill from a parked position, and control the vehicle legally

8. Check the parking brake, check and adjust as necessary the mirrors, and adjust as necessary seats, seat belts, etc.

9. Slow, straight, and smooth

10. Turn the left signal on before turning into the alley, back the car out of the alley, and stay on your side of the street

11. Traffic signs, controls devices, rights of way, lane markings, and turn signals

Special Services

1. Senior citizens, people with disabilities, and veterans

2. Free Rules of the Road Review Courses, license plates and parking placards, reduced license plate renewal fees, interpreter service for the deaf and hard of hearing when getting or renewing a driver`s license, and a free oral, in-person exam in place of a written exam

3. Free Rules of the Road Review Courses

4. License plates and parking placards are for people with disabilities, and reduced license plate renewal fees are for senior citizens and people with disabilities

5. The deaf or hard of hearing

6. They request it ahead of time

7. Someone with a reading or learning disability, or someone with a language barrier

8. Monday through Thursday
