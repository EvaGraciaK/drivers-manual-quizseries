3. 24 months

4. At least 9 consecutive months

5. At least 17 years and 3 months old

6. Pass the vision exam and the written exam

7. Until the driver`s permit expires

8. An adult instructor, a parent, a legal guardian, or other adult meeting the minimum requirements in the answer to question 9 below

Applying for an Illinois Driver`s License

1. It`s printed vertically with distinct features

2. An instruction permit, acceptable forms of identification, 50 hr driving log with a proper signature, Affidavit/Consent for Minor to Drive (if applicable), and Cooperative Driver Testing Certificate (if applicable)

4. The State Secretary must receive a notification that you successfully completed driver education, and the notation must appear on your driving record

5. You won`t have to pay an additional fee when applying for your driver`s license
